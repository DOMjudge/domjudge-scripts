DOMjudge - About the project

The DOMjudge project was started in 2004 at Study Association A-Eskwadraat at Utrecht University, The Netherlands. Development is still hosted here.

The authors are Jaap Eldering, Peter van de Werken, Thijs Kinkhorst Tobias Werth, and Nicky Gerritsen; with contributions from many others.

You can reach us through the development mailinglist at DOMjudge-devel@lists.A-Eskwadraat.nl.
